Color Schemes

Oct 5th 2016

First glance at the color theory is overwhelming. So much to learn for a beginner. The color red gives the user an uneasy feeling, green is the color of knowledge, yellow is best used for warnings. Seems like I can't just pick different tones of my favourite color. Diving in a little deeper I realize I'm also picking colors that clash, this styling process seems pretty challenging. However I did pick up a great tip for beginners. Use shades if you haven't mastered the proper use of colors. The color theory is definitely something I want to come back to, but for now I just want learn to code. Hello black white and various other shades of gray in between.
